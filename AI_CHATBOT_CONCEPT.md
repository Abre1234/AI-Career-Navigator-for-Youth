# AI Career Chatbot Concept

## Overview

The AI Career Navigator tool will be a simple AI-powered chatbot designed to provide career and education guidance to young people.

## Features

The chatbot will help users explore:

- Career paths and industries
- University programs
- Scholarship opportunities
- Digital skills learning resources

## Technology Approach

The prototype may use:

- Python
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Open-source AI models
- A simple web or messaging interface

## Future Vision

In the future, the tool could expand to include:

- Local scholarship databases
- Career recommendation systems
- Mentorship connections
- Multi-language support
