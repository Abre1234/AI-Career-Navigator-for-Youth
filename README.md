# AI Career Navigator for Youth

**Empowering young people with AI-driven career guidance, digital skills awareness, and education pathways.**

---

## Overview

Many young people, especially in developing regions, lack access to reliable career guidance, information about university programs, scholarships, and digital skill opportunities. As a result, students often make uninformed career choices or miss opportunities that could transform their future.

**AI Career Navigator for Youth** is a youth-led initiative that aims to bridge this gap by building a simple **AI-powered career guidance platform** combined with **community workshops and mentorship sessions**.

The project helps young people explore:

- Career paths in technology and other industries  
- University programs and scholarship opportunities  
- Digital skills and emerging technologies  
- Practical career planning strategies  

This initiative is designed to make **career guidance accessible, inclusive, and scalable** for youth communities.

---

## Problem Statement

Across many communities, young people face challenges such as:

- Limited access to professional career counseling  
- Lack of awareness about modern career opportunities  
- Minimal exposure to digital tools and AI technologies  
- Barriers for rural youth to access educational resources  

Without proper guidance, many students choose academic paths without understanding job market realities.

---

## Project Objectives

The project aims to:

1. Provide accessible **AI-powered career guidance** for youth.
2. Increase awareness of **education and scholarship opportunities**.
3. Introduce youth to **digital skills and emerging technologies**.
4. Support informed **career decision-making**.
5. Empower underserved communities with **knowledge and mentorship**.

---

## Key Activities

### 1. Youth Needs Assessment
Conduct surveys to understand the career information gaps among students.

### 2. AI Career Guidance Tool
Develop a simple **AI chatbot or digital platform** that can answer questions about:

- Career options  
- University programs  
- Scholarships  
- Technology skills  

### 3. Youth Workshops
Organize community workshops introducing:

- Career planning  
- Digital literacy  
- Artificial Intelligence basics  

### 4. Career Mentorship
Invite volunteers and professionals to share their career journeys and advice.

### 5. Community Outreach
Promote the project through youth networks, universities, and social media.

### 6. Monitoring and Feedback
Collect participant feedback and measure impact to improve the system.
