# Workshop Plan
AI Career Navigator for Youth

## Overview

The workshop component of the **AI Career Navigator for Youth** project is designed to provide young people with practical knowledge about career planning, digital skills, and emerging technology opportunities.

These workshops will introduce students to modern career pathways and help them explore how technology and artificial intelligence are transforming the job market.

The sessions will be interactive, practical, and accessible for youth with different educational backgrounds.

---

## Workshop Objectives

The workshops aim to:

- Help youth understand different career pathways and industries
- Introduce participants to digital literacy and emerging technologies
- Provide guidance on university programs and scholarships
- Build awareness about Artificial Intelligence and technology careers
- Support youth in making informed education and career decisions

---

## Target Participants

The workshops will primarily target:

- Secondary school students
- University students
- Youth in rural or underserved communities
- Young people interested in technology and innovation

Estimated participants per workshop: **25 – 30 youth**

Total expected beneficiaries: **100+ youth**

---

## Workshop Format

Each workshop will include:

- Interactive presentations
- Group discussions
- Demonstrations of digital tools
- Career guidance activities
- Q&A sessions with mentors

Participants will also be introduced to the **AI Career Navigator tool** to explore career information and opportunities.

---

## Workshop Topics

### 1. Introduction to Career Planning
- Understanding personal interests and strengths
- How to choose a career path
- Exploring different industries and professions

### 2. Digital Literacy and Future Skills
- Importance of digital skills
- Essential technology tools for students
- Online learning platforms

### 3. Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
- What is Artificial Intelligence?
- Real-world applications of AI
- Career opportunities in AI and data science

### 4. Education and Scholarship Opportunities
- University program selection
- Finding scholarship opportunities
- Preparing for academic success

### 5. Using the AI Career Navigator Tool
- Demonstration of the AI career chatbot
- How youth can explore career options using the platform

## Workshop Materials

Materials used during the workshops may include:

- Printed training guides
- Presentation slides
- Internet access for demonstrations
- Laptops or smartphones for participants
- Career exploration worksheets

---

## Expected Outcomes

By the end of the workshops, participants will:

- Understand different career options
- Gain awareness of digital and AI-related opportunities
- Learn how to access education and scholarship information
- Develop confidence in planning their future careers

---

## Monitoring and Evaluation

To measure the success of the workshops, the project will track:

- Number of participants trained
- Participant feedback and satisfaction
- Knowledge improvement through pre/post surveys
- Engagement with the AI Career Navigator tool

---

## Long-Term Impact

The workshop program aims to create a sustainable model for **youth career guidance and digital empowerment** that can be expanded to reach more communities in the future.
