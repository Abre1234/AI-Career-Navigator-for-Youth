# Impact Metrics

## Purpose

This document outlines how the impact of the AI Career Navigator for Youth project will be measured.

## Key Performance Indicators

### Youth Participation
Target: 100+ participants

### Workshops Conducted
Target: 3–4 workshops

### AI Tool Usage
Target: 200+ interactions with the career guidance tool

### Participant Satisfaction
Target: 80% positive feedback

## Evaluation Method

Impact will be measured through:

- Pre- and post-workshop surveys
- Participant feedback forms
- Tracking usage of the AI career guidance tool
