# Project Proposal
AI Career Navigator for Youth

## Project Summary

AI Career Navigator for Youth is a youth-led initiative that aims to improve access to career guidance, digital literacy, and education opportunities for young people. The project combines an AI-powered career guidance tool with community workshops to help youth explore career paths, scholarships, and future technology skills.

## The Challenge

Many young people, particularly in underserved communities, lack access to professional career counseling and reliable information about education and job opportunities. This often leads to uninformed academic choices and limited exposure to modern careers in technology and innovation.

## Proposed Solution

This project will develop a simple **AI-based career guidance tool** that allows youth to ask questions about careers, university programs, scholarships, and digital skills. The tool will be complemented by **in-person workshops and mentorship sessions** to help participants understand and use these opportunities effectively.

## Implementation Plan

The project will be implemented in four stages:

1. **Research & Needs Assessment**
   - Conduct surveys to identify career guidance gaps among youth.

2. **AI Tool Development**
   - Build a simple AI career guidance chatbot.

3. **Community Workshops**
   - Organize training sessions on career planning and digital skills.

4. **Mentorship & Feedback**
   - Connect youth with mentors and collect feedback to improve the platform.

## Expected Results

- At least **100 young people** will gain access to career guidance.
- Participants will learn about **education opportunities and digital skills**.
- Youth will be better prepared to make informed career decisions.

## Sustainability

The long-term vision is to expand the platform into a scalable digital resource that can support more youth communities and integrate additional features such as scholarship databases and mentorship networks.
