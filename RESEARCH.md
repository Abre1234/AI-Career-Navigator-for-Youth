# Research and Needs Assessment

## Purpose

Before implementing the AI Career Navigator for Youth project, a needs assessment will be conducted to understand the career guidance challenges faced by young people.

## Key Questions

The research will explore:

- Do students have access to career counseling?
- Are young people aware of scholarship opportunities?
- What careers are students most interested in?
- What digital skills do students want to learn?

## Method

A short survey and informal interviews will be conducted with youth participants to identify gaps in career guidance and digital knowledge.

## Expected Insights

The results will help guide the design of the AI career guidance tool and ensure the workshops address real needs within the community.
